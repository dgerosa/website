Applications are open for the Italian National PhD in AI:

[phd-ai-society.di.unipi.it/applications](https://phd-ai-society.di.unipi.it/applications/)

National PhDs are centralized programs, where applications are collected by a single institution (in this case, the University of Pisa), while students carry out their research at a range of participating institutions. In particular, this year there are two positions titled *"Accelerating Physics Research Using Artificial Intelligence"*, funded by INFN (the Italian National Institute for Nuclear Physics). These positions can be hosted by any research group affiliated with INFN, including my own. If you're interested in applying, please send me an email!
