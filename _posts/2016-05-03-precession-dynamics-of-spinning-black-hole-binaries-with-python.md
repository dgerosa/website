Here we present my numerical code [precession](https://github.com/dgerosa/precession), which implements our multi-timescale way to look at spinning black-hole binaries. The paper has a detailed description of the various functions as well as lots of examples.

**D. Gerosa**, M. Kesden.\
[Physical Review D 93 (2016) 124066](http://dx.doi.org/10.1103/PhysRevD.93.124066). [arXiv:1605.01067 [astro-ph.HE]](https://arxiv.org/abs/1605.01067).\
